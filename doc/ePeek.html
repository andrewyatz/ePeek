<static> gene()

gene sets the gene name for the next gene-based location gene-based locations have higher preference over coordinates-based locations So for example, using: gB.species("human").chr(13).from(35009587).to(35214822).gene("LINC00457"); will show the correct location even if the gene name is spelled wrong or is not recognized by Ensembl External gene names (BRCA2) and ensembl gene identifiers (ENSG00000139618) are both allowed.
